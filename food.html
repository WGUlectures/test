Taniti Food

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

Taniti has a small but growing food truck scene. Food trucks can be found in Taniti City and near the beach on the weekends. The food trucks serve a variety of foods, including local fish tacos, burgers, and ice cream.

Taniti has a small but growing craft beer scene. There are two breweries on the island that produce a variety of beers, including IPAs, stouts, and lagers. The breweries also have taprooms where visitors can sample the beers and learn about the brewing process.
