Taniti Lodging

Note: Average prices for lodging on Taniti are as follows:

  • Hostel: $30 per night
  • Bed and Breakfast: $80 per night
  • Hotel: $150 per night
  • Resort: $300 per night

Important: Due to the small size of the island, lodging is limited and fills up quickly during the peak tourist season (June through August). It is highly recommended that you make your lodging arrangements well in advance of your trip.

Note: All lodging on Taniti is currently at least 10 miles from the airport, so you will need to arrange transportation to your lodging before you arrive. See the Transportation page for more information.

Taniti has a wide variety of lodging that ranges from an inexpensive hostel to one large, four-star resort. There are many small, family-owned hotels and a growing number of bed and breakfasts. All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Taniti's only resort is located on the north end of the island and has a private beach, a spa, and several restaurants. The resort also offers a variety of activities, including snorkeling, scuba diving, and kayaking. The resort is popular with honeymooners and families with older children.

Taniti's hotels and bed and breakfasts are located throughout the island, but most are located in Taniti City and the surrounding area. The hotels and bed and breakfasts offer a variety of amenities, including free Wi-Fi, breakfast, and access to a pool. Many of the hotels and bed and breakfasts also offer tours and activities, such as hiking, snorkeling, and cultural experiences.

Taniti's hostel is located in Taniti City and offers dormitory-style accommodations. The hostel is popular with backpackers and budget travelers. The hostel has a communal kitchen, a lounge area, and a small outdoor space. The hostel also offers tours and activities, such as hiking, snorkeling, and cultural experiences.

Taniti has a small but growing vacation rental market. Vacation rentals can be found throughout the island, but most are located in Taniti City and the surrounding area. Vacation rentals range from small apartments to large houses and villas. Vacation rentals offer a variety of amenities, including kitchens, private pools, and access to the beach. Many vacation rentals also offer tours and activities, such as hiking, snorkeling, and cultural experiences.
