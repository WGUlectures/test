Welcome to Taniti!

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. While the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000. Until a recent increase in tourism, most the Tanitian economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture.

Taniti is a popular tourist destination for visitors from around the world. The island has a wide variety of lodging, dining, and entertainment options, and there are many activities to enjoy, including hiking, snorkeling, scuba diving, and surfing. The island is also known for its friendly and welcoming people.

Taniti is a great destination for a relaxing vacation, and it is also a popular destination for adventure seekers. Whether you are looking for a quiet beach getaway or an action-packed vacation, Taniti has something to offer everyone.
